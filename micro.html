About Us

Experience the best dish in CLASSIO
Classio Restaurant in Alandi Moshi is a casual dining spot that serves a variety of cuisines, including Chinese, North Indian, and South Indian.¹
Located at Gate 119, Gaikwad Property, Moshi Alandi, BRT Road, Bhosari, Pune, it's easily accessible for those in the area.²
Their menu is quite extensive, featuring a range of options such as soups, starters, main courses, breads,
rice and biryani, fried rice and noodles, snacks, accompaniments, and desserts.³
With over 18 soup options, 79 starters, and 113 main courses, you're sure to find something that suits your taste buds.
"Welcome to CLASSIO, where flavors come alive and memories are made!
Our chefs have crafted a menu that blends traditional Italian favorites with modern twists, using only the freshest ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience.
From our signature pizzas to our handcrafted pasta dishes, every bite is a taste of la dolce vita.
Join us for a cozy dinner, a night out with friends, or a special celebration – we can't wait to serve you!"
